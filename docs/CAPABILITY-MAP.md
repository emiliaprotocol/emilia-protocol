## The honest headline
On the protocol/cryptographic core (offline verification, multi-party quorum, formal proofs, tri-language conformance) EP is best-in-class for the government-oversight wedge. The product was never the blocker; the binding constraint is the **reliance event** ‚Äî an external auditor relying on a receipt in a live review.
