Control tested

Conclusion (sample): The sampled disbursement carries an independently-verifiable authorization receipt binding a named, device-verified human (key_class A) to the exact payment parameters. The control operated for this item. No exceptions noted.

Exception pattern (for contrast): had a sampled governed action produced no receipt — or a key_class C artifact where policy requires Class A, or challenge_binding: false — that would be recorded here as a control exception. Absence of a verifiable authorization is itself the finding.

Scope of assurance. Offline verification proves the artifact is authentic, intact, and bound to the exact action. It does not prove the underlying decision was correct, nor establish real-world identity beyond the enrollment layer, nor prove one-time consumption (server-state). Procedure to close those: request the auditee's consumption record and any signed revocation statement (EP-REVOCATION-v1, also offline-verifiable) for this receipt_id.
