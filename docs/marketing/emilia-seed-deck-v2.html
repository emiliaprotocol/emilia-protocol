01 · Why nowEMILIA Protocol
The gap

"A human approved this"
is a claim no one can check.

Today the proof lives in the operator's own editable logs — forgeable, backfillable, un-auditable by a regulator, court, or insurer without trusting the very party under review. That is the evidence gap. It is the whole company.
