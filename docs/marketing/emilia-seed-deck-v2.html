The inevitability

Software stopped asking permission.
It just acts now.

Agents already release payments, push deploys, change permissions, curtail datacenters, and command robots — at machine speed, with no human in the loop. The blast radius is real and irreversible. Every regulator, insurer, and board is now asking the same question, and no one can answer it: who authorized that, and can you prove it?
01 · Why this, why nowEMILIA Protocol
The gap

"A human approved this"
is a claim no one can check.

Today the proof lives in the operator's own editable logs — forgeable, backfillable, un-auditable by a regulator, court, or insurer without trusting the very party under review. Logs tell you what happened. They cannot prove who authorized it. That evidence gap is a category.
