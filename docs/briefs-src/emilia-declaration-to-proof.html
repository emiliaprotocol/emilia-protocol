A runnable composition of an externally evaluated RSL-MEDIA declaration, a bounded consent grant, a user-verified exact-action ceremony, and an executor that consumes the authorization once.

RSL-MEDIA declaration Standing terms, discovery, stable source identifier, purpose and territory conditions, and a path to obtain clearance. An external evaluator determines the operative declaration.
+
EMILIA exact-use evidence A signed, time-bounded grant plus a WebAuthn authorization over the material use fields. The executor rechecks current declared terms, verifies its pinned keys, and admits at most one action digest.

From standing declaration to one-time execution

01Discover

Resolve the declaration, ISRD, source digest, purpose, territory, and clearance endpoint.

02Grant

Sign the exact asset, use, purpose, territory, campaign, declaration digest, and expiry.

03Authorize

An enrolled approver completes user-verified WebAuthn over those material action fields.

04Consume

The executor rechecks the current declaration, reserves the action digest, performs once, and refuses mutation or replay.

Executable acceptance contract

Presented conditionResultReason
Exact declared use with all pinned evidenceEXECUTEAll constraints cover the signed action; one reservation wins.
Same action replayed or independently re-signedREFUSEThe action digest is already reserved or consumed.
Campaign, territory, purpose, or asset changedREFUSEThe signed grant no longer covers the action.
Action changed after human authorizationREFUSEThe WebAuthn challenge and action digest no longer match.
Declaration is prohibited, absent, or staleREFUSENo permissive fallback is inferred.

Exact boundary

  • Does not establish rights ownership or legal permission.
  • Does not prove the approver understood the display.
  • Does not assess output safety, accuracy, or quality.
  • Does not claim RSL-MEDIA conformance or endorsement.
  • Depends on a trusted external evaluator for the current declaration view.
  • RSL-MEDIA 1.0 is a draft that states it must not be used in production.
