# Emilia Eye Brief

## What's new (June 2026)
Emilia Eye composes cleanly into the new **Authorization Evidence Chain (EP-AEC)**: Eye's advisory flags *escalate* scrutiny, while the deterministic EP receipt — now independently re-verified by an outside implementer, with 3-language / 8-suite cross-language conformance — remains the gate. Eye never relaxes the gate; it only raises it.

Emilia Eye is the lightweight warning layer that flags when stricter EP trust controls should apply.

## What Eye does
- warns
- triages
- escalates

## What Eye does not do
- final trust decision
- public score
- blacklist
- generalized reputation engine

## Stack role
- Eye warns
- EP verifies
- Signoff owns
