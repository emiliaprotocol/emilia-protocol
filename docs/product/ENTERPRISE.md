# EP Enterprise

EP Enterprise is the hardened deployment and governance layer for regulated and high-assurance environments.

## What it includes

- private cloud / VPC deployment
- SSO / SCIM
- advanced RBAC
- delegated administration
- data residency
- enterprise evidence retention
- incident and forensics tooling
- regulator and auditor support

## Best-fit buyers

- government agencies
- financial institutions
- regulated enterprises
- organizations with named accountability requirements
