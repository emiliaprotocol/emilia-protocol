Operator Application

Close the loop on trust

Operators are the humans who make EP's constitutional guarantee real. When an appeal is filed, an operator reviews it — publicly, accountably, on the record.

Review appeals

When entities contest trust decisions, you evaluate the evidence and issue a ruling within 72 hours. Your decision is public and forms part of your own operator trust record.

Build operator record

Every ruling you make is scored. Response time, appeal outcomes, overturn rate, and consistency are tracked and published. Your operator record is itself a trust signal.

Govern the protocol

Operators are part of EP's governance layer. You help define what fair review looks like, propose policy changes, and participate in the ongoing development of the standard.

Requirements

EP operators must meet these baseline criteria before approval.

Real identity, public accountability

Operators are not anonymous. Your name and operator ID are public. Your decisions are auditable. This is the source of your authority — accountability, not anonymity.

Familiarity with the EP protocol

Read the Core Spec RFC and understand how trust profiles, receipts, and confidence levels work. You don't need to write code — you need to understand the evidence model.

72-hour response commitment

Appeals expire after 72 hours if unreviewed. You must commit to reviewing assigned appeals within that window. Volume is low at launch — expect 0–5 appeals per week.

No conflict of interest

You cannot review appeals from entities with which you have a financial or personal relationship. Conflicts must be declared and the appeal reassigned. Concealed conflicts are grounds for operator removal.

Apply now

Applications are reviewed manually. We'll respond within 5 business days. Early operators help shape the process — your input on the review workflow is part of the role.

Your application is stored securely. We don't share your information with third parties.

Application received

We'll review your application and respond within 5 business days. In the meantime, explore the protocol spec and get familiar with how EP evaluates trust evidence.
