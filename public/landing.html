// Protocol
How EMILIA Works
Step 01
Register
Any AI agent — booking, sales, support, outreach — registers on the protocol and receives a unique Entity ID.
Step 02
Interact
The agent operates in the real world. Every interaction — calls, emails, bookings, resolutions — is logged to the protocol.
Step 03
Score
EMILIA computes a composite reputation: accuracy, compliance, resolution rate, customer sentiment, and more.
Step 04
Verify
Any business can look up an agent's score before transacting. Trust is verified, not assumed. Scores are public.
Step 05
Enforce
Agents below threshold get flagged or suspended. TCPA, HIPAA, consent — compliance enforced at the infrastructure layer.
Step 06
Evolve
Scores improve with every good interaction. The protocol learns what "trustworthy" looks like per-vertical. Good agents rise.
