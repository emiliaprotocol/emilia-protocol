# EMILIA Protocol Logos

Brand colors: `#0C0A09` ink, `#B08D35` gold (signature accent), `#0B1220` navy, `#FAFAF9` off-white.

## Logo Files

### `emilia-protocol.svg` (60x60)
**Main EP logo mark.** The EMILIA "E": gold vertical stem + gold middle arm, with the top/bottom arms in `currentColor` (ink on light surfaces, white on dark — set the parent `color`). This is the leading letter of the EMILIA wordmark and the primary brand icon. The full wordmark lockup ("EMILIA" with this colored E) and a self-contained tile favicon live in `public/` (`logo-wordmark.png`, `favicon.svg`).

### `emilia-eye.svg` (200x60)
**EMILIA EYE product logo.** Spells "EYE" with the last E horizontally mirrored. Green monochrome. Represents AI-generated content detection and surveillance from both sides.

### `ep-handshake.svg` (200x60)
**EP Handshake product logo.** Two interlocking chevrons (><) in blue. Represents binding, verification, and cryptographic locking between AI and human parties.

### `ep-signoff.svg` (200x60)
**EP Signoff product logo.** Checkmark inside a shield. Green check, blue shield outline. Represents human accountability, approval authority, and final sign-off.

### `ep-cloud.svg` (200x60)
**EMILIA Gate Cloud product logo.** Minimalist cloud outline with a geometric lock inside. Blue cloud, green lock. Represents managed Gate operations and private deployment.

### `ep-stack.svg` (300x200)
**Full stack diagram.** Three-layer vertical visualization: Eye (warns) -> Handshake (verifies) -> Signoff (owns). Uses IBM Plex Mono for labels. Works as a standalone explanatory diagram.

## Usage Guidelines

- The primary mark (`emilia-protocol.svg`) works on both dark (`#0B1220`) and light (`#FAFAF9`) backgrounds — set the parent `color` to ink on light, white on dark; the gold stays fixed.
- Do not add drop shadows or decorative effects to the mark itself.
- Maintain original aspect ratios when scaling.
- Minimum clear space: 1x the height of the mark on all sides.
- Gold `#B08D35` is the only accent color — keep the stem + middle arm gold; never recolor the gold.
- The older product logos below (`emilia-eye`, `ep-handshake`, `ep-signoff`, `ep-cloud`, `ep-stack`) predate the current brand and are pending a refresh to the ink/gold system.
