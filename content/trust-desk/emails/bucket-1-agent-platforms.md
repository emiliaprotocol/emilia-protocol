- Headcount 25–300, Seed to Series B
- "Enterprise" or "for banks" in the site copy

**Timing signal (any one of)**
- Announced a new enterprise customer in last 90 days

**"What's your price?"** → don't get drawn into a pricing discussion in email. "Packet is $24,500 fixed. Full scope on aitrustdesk.com/pricing. 15-min call is the fastest way to know if it fits your specific review."

**"Who are your customers?"** (early days) → "We're in the first cohort — I'll send anonymized case studies as they close. In the meantime I'll share my security/compliance background and what the 5 policy templates look like."
