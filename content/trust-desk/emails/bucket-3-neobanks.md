# Cold email — Bucket 3: AI-native fintech infrastructure / neo-fintech vendors

**Target profile**
- AI-native financial infrastructure (embedded banking, treasury ops, corporate cards, B2B payments, receivables automation)
- Selling into mid-market and enterprise finance orgs (CFOs, controllers, treasury departments)
- Often Series A-B, 50–250 employees
- Core product involves AI summarizing, routing, approving, or posting financial data
- Buyer's security team is usually the CFO's IT security + a contracted vCISO

**Why they close faster than Bucket 1 or 2**
Mid-market CFO buyers have shorter cycles than bank risk teams. A stuck deal is measured in weeks, not quarters. The trust-page URL is enough to unstick most of their deals — they don't need full regulatory-grade responses, they need something the CFO can forward to their IT security partner.
